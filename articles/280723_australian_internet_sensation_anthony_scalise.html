Australian Internet Sensation Anthony Scalise Challenges Boxing Champion George Kambosos to a Showdown!

Controversial internet personality and entrepreneur, Anthony Scalise, has set his sights on professional boxer George Kambosos.

Scalise, known for his political right wing affiliations within Australia, USA and Italy and vast online following, isn't one to shy away from making headlines, and his latest call-out has everyone talking.

The spark that ignited this was the contentious decision in the IBO World Lightweight Title match, where George Kambosos emerged victorious over Maxi Hughes in a decision that left fans divided and furious.

Anthony Scalise, who wasted no time in voicing his outrage on social media, accusing Kambosos of robbing Hughes of his hard-earned title.

With his considerable online clout, Scalise laid down the gauntlet, challenging Kambosos to a face-off in the boxing ring and putting the belt on the line.

Expecting a response from the boxing champion, Scalise was taken aback when George Kambosos opted to remain silent.

In a surprising move, Kambosos blocked Scalise on Twitter/X, seemingly unwilling to engage in a war of words with the internet celebrity.
