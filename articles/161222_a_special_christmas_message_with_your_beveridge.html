A Special Christmas Message with Your Beveridge

(photo courtesy of Sumpton's Gold Rush Gazette)

Jack May - Originally published on 16/12/2022 for the Charters Towers newspaper, Sumpton's Gold Rush Gazette.

Charters Towers Mayor Frank Beveridge has given a special Christmas message to locals.

Mr Beveridge wished locals a joyful Christmas on behalf of the council.

“On behalf of the council we wish everyone a safe and happy Christmas and anyone who's traveling, travel safely,” he said.

Mr Beveridge also said Australia was a great place to be this time of year due to the uncertainty in other parts of the globe.

“Obviously this Christmas we can reflect on what’s happening in Ukraine and other parts of the world and see just how lucky Australia is to have stability,” he said.

As council winds down for Christmas, Mr Beveridge discussed the process of getting ready for a small break.

“We have a staff gathering before everyone goes away for the break, which is nice,” he said.

“It's just a time to be with everyone and to be really thankful about how lucky we are.”
